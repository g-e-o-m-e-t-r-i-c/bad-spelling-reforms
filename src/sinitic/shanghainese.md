# shanghainese

## goals

- [x] concise
- [x] reversible
- [x] phonetic-leaning (opposed to **phonemic**)
- [ ] ascii-compatible
- [ ] intuitive

## writing

| initials | lb  | alv | pl  | vl  | gl  |
| -------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ns       | m   | n   | ny  | ng  |     |

- pre-glottalised initials add a _h_ after the letter. thus, /ˀȵ/ is **_nyh_**.
